About

We are the Generative AI Research Group located in the School of Computing and FSE Centre of Frontier AI Research at Macquarie University. Our group focuses on advancing generative AI technologies for real-world applications, including:

  • Diffusion models/rectified flows, image autoregressive models
  • Generative model distillation methods
  • Applications in image/video editing, large language models
